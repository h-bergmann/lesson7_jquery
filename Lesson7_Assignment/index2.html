Guacamole

Ingredients

  • 2 avocados
  • 1 smallonion, finely chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 ripe tomato, chopped
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Peel and mash avocados in a mixing bowl

Stir in onion

Stir in tomato

Stir in lime juice, salt, and pepper

Season with remaining lime juice and salt and pepper to taste

Chill for half an hour to blend the flavors
